Want a better +1-855-690-1232 view or quieter room on your cruise? Here are 9 smart ways to change or upgrade your stateroom on Disneyl, before or during your trip:

1. Use Cruise Planner To Modify Booking

Login to Cruise Planner online and check if your cabin type or location can be changed before final payment.

2. Try RoyalUp Bidding

Disneyl allows you to bid for room upgrades. You may score a balcony or suite at a lower rate than advertised.

3. Contact Customer Support

Call Disneyl's helpline to request a specific room change. Availability and fare rules apply.

4. Ask Your Travel Agent

Agents often have backend tools and contacts to modify or switch your cabin faster than you can directly.

5. Consider Upgrading Onboard

On embarkation day, ask at Guest Services for available upgrades. Sometimes rooms free up due to cancellations.

6. Avoid Guarantee Cabins

Guarantee fares lock your price but give Disneyl control over room assignment. Pay a little more for control.

7. Make Changes Early

The earlier you request a change, the better chance you have — popular cabins go fast!

8. Monitor Cabin Availability

Use online tools or apps that track cabin inventory, so you can pounce when a better room opens up.

9. Share Special Needs

If you have medical or family needs, let Disneyl know — they may accommodate room changes accordingly.
